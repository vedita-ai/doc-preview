Even though, as observed hereinabove, some of the invoices filed by the Claimant have not been considered by the Respondent in the statement filed on 30.04.2024 to show the total value of invoices for assessment fee, however, in the absence of any stand taken by the Claimant in respect to these invoices in his statement of claim, and also since the claim of the Claimant is based solely on the email of 18.02.2019 and the reconciliation sheet filed along with the same, the Tribunal holds that these invoices raised by the Claimant on account of assessment fee were covered in the total Coordinator's share of assessment fee was Rs.99,69,747/-, as submitted by the Respondent.
