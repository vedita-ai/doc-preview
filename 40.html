In view of the discussion made hereinabove, outstanding sum payable by the Respondent to the Claimant was Rs.12,28,107/-. There is no contract between the parties for the payment of any interest to the Claimant on account of late or non-payment by the Respondent. The Claimant is therefore not entitled to any pre-litigation interest. At the same time, there is no bar to the payment of interest as well. The Tribunal is of the view that the Respondent, who is a small entrepreneur, has been illegally and unjustifiably denied the payment of the sums due to him by the Respondent Company. Therefore, this Tribunal holds the Claimant to be entitled to pendent-lite interest @ 12% per annum on the aforesaid balance outstanding sum with effect from the date when the present arbitration proceedings were invoked by legal notice dated 06.09.2021 till the date of the passing of the present Award.
